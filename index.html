Location

As the smallest country in terms of both land mass and population (764), Vatican City does not have a capital. The country's coordinates, though, are 41.9029° N, 12.4534° E.
The Vatican is landlocked and completely surrounded by Italy. It is historically part of Rome.
The Vatican City has one timezone, Central European Summer Time.
The Vatican is typically abbreviated as VA or VAT.
The spoken language is Italian, and the practiced religion is Catholicism.
Arguably the most important leader in the Vatican is the Pope. The current pope is Pope Francis, who has been pope since 2013. He turns 88 this December.


Vatican City is centered around the catholic church. It is urban and landlocked by Rome, Italy.
